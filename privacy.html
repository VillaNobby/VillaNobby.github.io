Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Villa Nobby ("we", "us") operates villanobby.com, the website for our guest suite in Miami, Queensland. We respect your privacy and handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles.

What we collect

  • Booking enquiries — when you submit the enquiry form we collect your name, email address, mobile number, requested dates and any details you share about your stay.
  • Guest list subscriptions — if you join our mailing list we collect your email address.
  • Website analytics — we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, device type, general location). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry, confirm availability and arrange your stay.
  • To send occasional updates and offers if you have joined the guest list — you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.
  • To improve the website and our guest experience.

Who processes it

We use a small number of trusted services to run the site: Formspree (enquiry form delivery), MailerLite (guest list emails), Google Analytics (site statistics) and GitHub Pages (website hosting). Each processes data only as needed to provide their service. We never sell your personal information.

Retention & security

Enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to manage your booking and our records. Mailing list details are kept until you unsubscribe. Payment is never taken through this website.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct or delete your personal information at any time — simply reply to any email from us, use the enquiry form, or message us via our Airbnb listing.
