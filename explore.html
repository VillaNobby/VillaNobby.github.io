Things to Do

The best of the Gold Coast,
right at your door

North Burleigh Beach
Your closest beach — a quiet, uncrowded stretch of sand perfect for morning swims and sunset walks. A local favourite.
800m · 10 min walk
Burleigh Head National Park
Stunning coastal rainforest walk with panoramic ocean views. The oceanview trail loop takes about 45 minutes and is well worth it.
2.5km · 5 min drive
Miami Marketta
The Gold Coast's famous night markets — street food from around the world, live music, and a vibrant atmosphere. Open Wed, Fri & Sat nights.
1.5km · 3 min drive
Village Markets
Trendy weekend markets at Burleigh Heads with local designers, handmade goods, fashion, art and delicious food stalls. First & third Sunday of the month.
2.5km · 5 min drive
Surfing & Lessons
World-class breaks at Burleigh, Currumbin and Snapper Rocks. Multiple surf schools offer beginner lessons at nearby beaches.
Various locations nearby
Tallebudgera Creek
A calm, crystal-clear tidal creek ideal for swimming, kayaking and stand-up paddleboarding. Great for all ages and perfect on warmer days.
3km · 6 min drive
Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary
Meet koalas, kangaroos and native Australian wildlife at this iconic Gold Coast attraction. Includes daily bird feeding and live shows.
5km · 8 min drive
Brewery Trail
Miami is the craft beer capital of the Gold Coast. Walk to Roji Cat, Precinct Brewery, Black Hops and Hard Fizz — all within 12 minutes on foot.
450m–850m · All walkable
Gold Coast Oceanway
A 36km coastal walking and cycling path stretching from Coolangatta to Main Beach. Join from North Burleigh for stunning ocean views in both directions.
Access from 800m · Walk or cycle
Springbrook National Park
Explore ancient rainforest, waterfalls, and the famous Natural Bridge glow worm cave. A beautiful day trip into the Gold Coast hinterland.
35km · 45 min drive
James Street & Burleigh Village
Boutique shopping, independent stores, homewares and fashion. Browse at your own pace through the Gold Coast's most curated retail precinct.
2km · 5 min drive
Whale Watching (Seasonal)
Spot humpback whales on their annual migration along the Gold Coast from June to November. Watch from Burleigh Headland or book a tour.
Jun–Nov · Burleigh Headland free
