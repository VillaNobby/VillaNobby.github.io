What's On

Late July 2026 on the Gold Coast

The UCI BMX World Championships race into their final days in Brisbane this week, while closer to home the winter calendar keeps things cosy — hands-on dumpling evenings at HOTA on Wednesdays, art-inspired dining at Palette, and starlit Celestial Nights at Sanctuary Cove from Friday. Looking ahead: Heathers the Musical opens at HOTA on 30 July, GreazeFest and the International Cake Show fill the first weekend of August, and later in the month brings new theatre at La Boite, the songbook of Barbra Streisand at Twin Towns, and Vivaldi at QPAC.

HOTA Farmers & Artisan Markets
Weekly farmers market with fresh local produce, artisan goods, specialty coffee, and live music in the HOTA arts precinct. A Gold Coast Sunday morning favourite.

Last Sunday of each month, 8 AM — 2 PM
Burleigh Heads Beachside Markets
The longest-running art and craft market on the Gold Coast, going strong for over 30 years. Local artisans, food vendors, and live music in a beachside setting at Justin's Park.

Local growers and artisan producers come together every Saturday morning. Fresh produce, bulk foods, locally made oils, honey, fresh flowers, and specialty coffee.
