The Location

Nestled in Miami's most
desirable beachside pocket

Miami is one of the Gold Coast's best-kept secrets — a relaxed beachside neighbourhood with stunning coastline, leafy streets, and a thriving local café and dining scene. Walk to North Burleigh Beach in minutes, grab a coffee from one of the many nearby cafés, or explore the famous Burleigh Heads headland just a short drive away. It's the perfect base to experience the Gold Coast like a local.
